## SEO Optimization Checklist for Each Post

- [ ] **Title Tag:** 50-60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters, compelling call-to-action
- [ ] **Headings:** H1 for title, H2 for sections, proper hierarchy
- [ ] **Internal Links:** 3-5 links to relevant tools
- [ ] **Word Count:** 1,500-2,500 words
- [ ] **Images:** At least 1-2 relevant images
- [ ] **Keywords:** Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] **URL Slug:** Descriptive, includes primary keyword
- [ ] **Related Posts:** Link to 2-3 related blog posts
- [ ] **Call-to-Action:** Encourage tool usage or newsletter signup
