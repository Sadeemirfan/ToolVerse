## Tips for Domain Success

1. **Avoid Hyphens** - Domains without hyphens are easier to remember
2. **Keep it Short** - Shorter domains are more memorable
3. **Use Keywords** - Include relevant keywords for SEO
4. **Check Availability** - Ensure social media handles are available too
5. **Plan for Growth** - Choose a domain that works long-term
6. **Secure Early** - Popular domains get taken quickly
