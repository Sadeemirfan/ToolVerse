## 7. STEP-BY-STEP APPLICATION PROCESS

### Before Applying (Week 1-4)

1. **Finalize Content** (Week 1)
   - Add 4-9 more blog articles (total 10-15)
   - Ensure all content is original and high-quality
   - Proofread for grammar and spelling

2. **Optimize Technical Setup** (Week 2)
   - Verify HTTPS is working
   - Test mobile responsiveness
   - Check page speed
   - Submit sitemap to Google Search Console

3. **Build Initial Traffic** (Week 3-4)
   - Share tools on social media
   - Promote blog posts
   - Target at least 100-500 monthly visitors
   - Monitor analytics

### Applying for AdSense (Week 4-5)

1. **Go to AdSense.google.com**
2. **Click "Sign Up Now"**
3. **Enter your domain name** (e.g., toolverse.com)
4. **Provide personal information**
   - Full name
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Payment method
5. **Add AdSense code** to your website
6. **Wait for approval** (typically 24-48 hours)

### After Approval (Week 5+)

1. **Place ad units** on your site
   - Homepage banner ads
   - Tool page sidebar ads
   - Blog post in-content ads
2. **Monitor performance** in AdSense dashboard
3. **Optimize ad placement** for revenue
4. **Continue creating content** for growth
5. **Track earnings** and traffic metrics
